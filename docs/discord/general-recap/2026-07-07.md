# Community Recap — #general

**July 7, 2026** | 12:22 AM UTC – 9:23 AM UTC

## From Cassie

Cassie answered several technical questions from the community:

- **Epoch length (720 vs 360 frames):** Longer epoch reduces overall churn intensity.
- **Halt risk shards:** The network will go live once all 49 halt risk shards are cleared; there is no redivision into 3098/4096 sub-shards unless coverage warrants it (presently it does not).
- **Slow frame times:** A lagging archive node is causing timeouts, which sometimes stack because the proposer selection randomly picks it repeatedly. She is working on getting it caught up.
- **Next steps after halt risk cleared:** The network unhalts, shard frame proofs start landing and rewards accumulate, and other expected operations begin.
- **Bandwidth usage:** Higher bandwidth is expected during correct network functioning as provers engage in consensus.

## Moderation Incident

TrustX posted a promotion (apparently a wallet-connecting site) and was asked by agwm_2014 not to share it in main channels. After a brief argument about authority and openness, agwm_2014 gave a timeout. Several Chinese-language community members defended the moderation, citing security concerns, while others argued the community should remain open to all projects. agwm_2014 clarified the action was to protect users’ funds and noted the promoter can use dedicated channels (e.g., Quorum, separate Discord) freely.

## Node Performance & Bandwidth

Otter reported that his Mac Mini (Mac14,3) is now running well with the updated version after months of inactivity, though bandwidth and data traffic requirements are high. VELVIL VR noted bandwidth spikes in the current version and asked if the official .25 release will stabilize usage. Cassie indicated the increase is normal when the network is functioning correctly.
