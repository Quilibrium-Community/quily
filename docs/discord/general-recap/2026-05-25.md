# Community Recap — #general

**May 25, 2026** | 1:11 AM UTC – 9:48 AM UTC

## Shard Health Updates

The community discussed the ongoing improvements in shard health statuses:
- Halt risk shards are decreasing (down to 83 at time of discussion), moving first to "warning" status before potentially reaching "healthy"
- Version 0.23 appears to be responsible for recent rapid improvements in shard health
- Nodes with workers in healthy shards on v0.22 that update to v0.23 may temporarily leave healthy shards to support halt risk shards

## Mainnet Launch Requirements

neon🦦 clarified the conditions for Mainnet 2.1 launch:
- The network can launch once all "halt" state shards are moved to "warning"
- However, insufficient online nodes could cause the launched Mainnet to freeze until more workers join
