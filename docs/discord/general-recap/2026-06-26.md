# Community Recap — #general

**June 26, 2026** | 1:17 AM UTC – 10:14 AM UTC

## From Cassie
Cassie shared the current status of the **.25 update**:
- ✅ Revised minimum shard height to 1 for the QUIL token shard (reducing from 4096 divisions to 64)
- ✅ Reviewed scale-up/down shard functionality
- ⏳ Adding aggressive caching / reducing read pressure
- ⏳ Separating materialization/commit path completely from the consensus path

She also noted that the recent coverage halt was caused by evictions from the .25 work, leading to network instability as over 2/3 of workers dropped. She advised users to simply leave their standard nodes running and the network will self-correct.

## TrustX / OTC Platform Controversy
A new member, **TrustX**, introduced an OTC platform built for Quilibrium (using Ethereum as a settlement layer). The announcement was met with immediate skepticism. Community members (Poorest, lewsid2.0, clarity, Miggy, and others) pointed out multiple red flags: ChatGPT-style responses, refusal to provide team details or source code before launch, lack of registered business, and striking similarities to the earlier **QGame** scam. Cassie clarified that the project is not affiliated with Q Inc.

TrustX eventually offered to let “trusted community leaders” review the code and participate in a multisig, but remained evasive. Calls for a **known scams directory** and a separate “ideas” vetting channel were raised by Miggy, lewsid2.0, and others. The general consensus was that the project needed far more transparency before anyone should connect a wallet.
