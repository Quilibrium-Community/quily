# Community Recap — #general

**June 16, 2026** | 12:54 AM UTC – 10:53 AM UTC

## Release Progress and v0.24 Update

The community discussed the upcoming v0.24 release, with many noting a shift in mood compared to a year ago. Cassie [LEAD DEV] provided a detailed update on three remaining issues for the release:

1. **Black Swan report**: A potential issue with reinitializing workers and leaving shards, though Cassie noted it might be timing-related rather than a real bug.
2. **Unstable archive node**: Cassie plans to resolve this.
3. **Explorer update**: The explorer will be rewritten in Rust to prevent frequent crashes.

Later, Cassie confirmed that a new update to the .24 branch had been pushed, addressing the Black Swan report. The update is mandatory for nodes.

## Community Reflections on FUD and Progress

Several members reflected on the project’s journey. Cassie noted that a year ago, none of the Q web services were publicly accessible, and many features (KLEARU, MetaVM, Quorum Mobile) were heavily FUDed. She emphasized that the evidence now contradicts the earlier skepticism. Others echoed that the FUD period was intense but that Cassie’s consistent delivery has silenced critics. The mood was described as more optimistic than in the past.

## Spiritual and Philosophical Discussion

A lengthy off-topic conversation emerged about religious and philosophical texts. ## Other Mentions

- **Quorum Test Space**: LaMat promoted a test space for Quorum Messenger, providing an invite link for mobile users.
- **Secret chat feature**: Mr.Quil asked about ephemeral messages in Quorum. Cassie explained that while possible, open-source clients cannot prevent recipients from ignoring deletion, similar to screenshot risks.
- **QGame**: A community-built gaming platform was discussed; it is not yet Quilibrium-native but aims to integrate as the network grows.
