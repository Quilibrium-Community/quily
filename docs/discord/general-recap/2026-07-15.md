# Community Recap — #general

## From Cassie
Cassie clarified that "launch" means different things to different people: to some, it's everything working as proposed; to others, it's a vibes-based evaluation tied to price action. She confirmed she is now on the other side of analyzing node workers' performance — fixing findings directly related to consensus issues, sync slowdowns, and mesh formation difficulties. She stated this round of debugging is fundamentally different from previous ones, involving either fixing performance areas or swapping out libraries with faster versions.

## Network Status and Launch Discussions
Community members debated whether the Quilibrium network is already launched. TULFIX noted that QStorage works (e.g., deploying a website), while CHUNCH argued that core features — deploying apps, transferring coins, bridging — are not yet functional. Finn❄❄❄ clarified that the network is live, but the coin shards are not running properly; everything else (Quorum, QStorage) runs on the existing network. Cassie’s earlier roadmap (shared by Finn) outlined: shard-out completes, then stability monitoring, then LLTI collectible on mainnet, then bridge goes live. The conversation highlighted that while the network is operational, all proposed features are not yet available, leading to mixed perceptions of "launch."
