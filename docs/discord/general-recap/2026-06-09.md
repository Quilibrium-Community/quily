# Community Recap — #general

**June 9, 2026** | 12:30 AM UTC – 8:51 AM UTC

## MetaVM Update and Target Market

Aza asked about the target market for the recent MetaVM proof-of-EVM breakthrough. Cassie [LEAD DEV] responded:

- **Potential users**: Ethereum itself (for Beam Chain), bridges seeking trustless cross-chain approaches, and any cross-chain interoperability projects.
- **Outreach**: She is already talking with teams who find it immediately useful. A Farcaster post (https://farcaster.xyz/cassie/0x59e665e8) serves as a prototype for upcoming Twitter messaging.
- **Documentation**: The repo README gives a decent overview but is developer-focused; more documentation will follow.
- **Marketing push**: Will ramp up after shard stabilization and node earnings are smoothed out. "Would be fiercely unwise to jump in on that just yet."

## Node Update v0.23 Rollout

Adoption of the new release was gradual. Early reports showed only 51–120 nodes online, later reaching 35.3% on v0.23 per Quilscan. Cassie noted that some distros (e.g., Ubuntu 22.04) lack a clean upgrade path due to glibc version requirements. Community members monitored halt risk and healthy shard counts, with alcohol explaining that a halt risk of 0 sustained for 360+ frames triggers eviction and resharding. By late morning, halt risk remained stable and healthy shards were at 2.430.

## Quilscan Agent v1.1.0

A new update was released for the Quilscan node management console. Key additions: optimized qclient commands, a Manage tab, local prover allocations view, shard actions (Join/Leave/Pause/Resume etc.), and signature verification for qclient downloads.
