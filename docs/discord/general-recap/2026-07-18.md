# Community Recap — #general

## Community Recap: July 18, 2026

### Exchange Labeling Issue
The main discussion centered on an incident where an exchange (presumably Kraken) labeled a wallet as belonging to the "Quilibrium team" and demanded proof that it wasn't. The wallet in question had received 2.5 million QUIL tokens from the mint (which paused April 15, 2025), paired them with 140 ETH for liquidity, reinvested gains, and sold all 15 million QUIL after two years. Community members expressed frustration:

- blacks1ne noted the wallet's activity "screams degenerate crypto bro" — it was funded via Coinbase Prime and immediately used for online gambling.
- Several users suggested the ask is logically impossible: as sup said, no one can "prove lack of ownership of an address."

### From Cassie [LEAD DEV]
Cassie acknowledged the situation, stating the team is willing to provide information but that the exchange made an "impossible ask" — you cannot disprove ownership of an address. She noted a different support agent has since taken over the thread, seemingly realized the request was unreasonable, and said they're "looking into it." She also called the wallet's Coinbase Prime funding source "weird."

When questioned about brand damage, Cassie said the team will respond if needed, but is currently awaiting guidance on how to satisfy the exchange's request.

### Community Reactions
