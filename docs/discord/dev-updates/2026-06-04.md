## LaMat — 2:06 PM UTC

**Dev update from Cassie **

- .23 has been undergoing a lot of testing and revision. I continue to collect feedback and logs, and improve to prepare us for release
- quorum mobile has improved a _lot_, though Android users are especially still reporting issues. It's a bit tough at times to adequately address Android because the emulator fucking sucks, but some folks have been giving adb logcat data which has helped a lot
- klearu has seen a few updates, supporting additional training modes, cross model latent space translation and dissection/reassembly (think frankenmodels), improved sparsification, new image models, inpainting support, and a transformer driven image model
- MetaVM is about to receive a major update that has vastly improved the proof structure of EVM driven zkps
- F(x), our aws lambda compatible service (with some additional support for cloudflare workers and vercel style deployments) is going live likely end of week, but Relational (RDS/Keyspaces compatible APIs) and Compute (EC2) will be going live
- Quorum web is being lifted to support everything quorum mobile has
