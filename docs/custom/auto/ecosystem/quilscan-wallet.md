# QuilScan Wallet

**Part of the official Quilibrium ecosystem.** Relation to the network: Plugs into Quilibrium (an app or tool that connects to Quilibrium but runs on its own servers).

**A browser-extension wallet for `$QUIL`.** The QuilScan Wallet holds keys, signs transactions, and bridges `$QUIL` on Quilibrium to `$wQUIL` on Ethereum from one extension, without running a Quilibrium node.

It installs from the **Chrome Web Store** or from **wallet.quilscan.com**, and is part of QuilScan, a broader set of tools the same team builds around the Quilibrium network, alongside the QuilScan Agent.

**A note on trust.** The QuilScan team has been around since the early days of Quilibrium and contributed a lot to the network. The community has come to trust them. They are, however, **still anonymous, and the wallet itself is closed source**. That means no one outside the team can read what the extension actually does with your seed phrase and keys, or verify it independently. Treat it like any closed-source wallet from a team you cannot identify: useful, worth using if it fits your needs, **but not the place to keep more than you would be comfortable losing**.
