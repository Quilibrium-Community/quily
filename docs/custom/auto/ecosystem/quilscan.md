# QuilScan

**Part of the official Quilibrium ecosystem.** Relation to the network: Plugs into Quilibrium (an app or tool that connects to Quilibrium but runs on its own servers).

**A real-time explorer for the Quilibrium network.**

The QuilScan Dashboard shows **network-level stats** (global node distribution, consensus frames, peer geography, version breakdown), **shard coverage and health**, **individual node and prover details** (status, version, shard assignments, ring distribution, seniority), and a full **QNames browser** with ownership, transfer history, governance points, top holders, and the marketplace with live auctions and resale listings.

Built and maintained by the same team behind the QuilScan Wallet and the QuilScan Agent.
