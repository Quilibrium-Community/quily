# ZapMe

**Part of the official Quilibrium ecosystem.** Relation to the network: Plugs into Quilibrium (an app or tool that connects to Quilibrium but runs on its own servers).

**A borderless communications app that combines a decentralized social network, eSIM connectivity in 150+ countries, and global phone numbers, all from a single mobile app.** ZapMe positions itself as a communications super-app: own your identity and your data on the social side, and skip roaming charges and SIM swaps on the telecom side.

The telecom layer is built on **eSIM** technology and lets you hold multiple active phone numbers on one device, receive SMS and OTP codes without activating roaming, and top up data from inside the app. There are no contracts and no lock-in. The 150+ country coverage is aimed at international students, business travellers, digital nomads, expats, and anyone who needs a local number somewhere they don't physically live yet.

The social side, **De-So (Decentralized Social)**, is a user-controlled platform built around `@handle` usernames with integrated calling, video, and messaging. The idea is to take the basic shape of a social/messaging app, but flip the ownership: you control your identity and your data instead of handing them to a centralized platform. The app includes the `$ZAP` token with staking, and a roadmap toward creator monetisation (ZapMe LIVE) and borderless payments (ZapPay).

ZapMe uses **Quilibrium for media storage**: images and videos uploaded through the app are hosted on Quilibrium's decentralized storage rather than on a traditional centralized provider. It's a focused, practical integration: the app's telecom and social logic run on ZapMe's own infrastructure, while the media layer takes advantage of Quilibrium's properties.

Available today on **iOS and Android**, with 500 free minutes for ZapMe-to-ZapMe calls and pre-sale activity around the `$ZAP` token underway.
